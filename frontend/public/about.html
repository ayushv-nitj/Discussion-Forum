🧭 User Instructions: How to Use the Discussion Forum

Welcome aboard! 🚀 Whether you're here to start a discussion or join one, this guide will help you get the most out of our forum. Let’s walk you through everything step by step:

  • 🔐 Login to Get Started
    1. Click the “Login to Continue” button on the homepage.
    2. Sign in securely using your Google account.
    3. Your profile (name and picture) will appear once logged in.


  • 💬 Start a New Discussion
    1. Use the “Start a New Discussion” section at the top.
    2. Enter a meaningful title for your thread.
    3. Optionally, you can add a description to give others context.
    4. Click “Create” and your thread will appear below.
    5. ✅ Only logged-in users can create discussions.


  • 🧵 Join Existing Threads
    1. Scroll through the list of discussion threads under “Discussion Threads.”
    2. Click on any thread title to enter and view messages.
    3. Participate by typing a message and clicking “Send.”


  • ✏️ Edit or Delete Your Messages
    1. You can edit or delete your own messages at any time.
    2. Click the ✏️ Edit or 🗑️ Delete button below your message.
    3. When editing, press ✔ to save or ✖ to cancel.
    4. 🛡 Only your own messages can be edited or deleted.


  • 🧼 Manage Your Threads
    1. You’ll see a delete button (🗑) next to threads you created.
    2. You can remove your thread anytime if you feel it’s no longer needed.
    3. 🛑 You cannot delete threads started by other users.


  • 🌙 Switch Themes
    1. Click the 🌙 / ☀️ button at the top-right to toggle between dark and light mode for better viewing comfort.


  • 🌐 Explore More
    1. Visit the About Us section to learn about our mission.


    • 🙌 Final Tips

    • Be respectful and collaborative.

    • Stay on-topic in discussions.

    • Report any issues or suggestions via GitHub.


    Happy discussing! 💡✨
